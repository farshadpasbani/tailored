# AI Engineer, Trustworthy Systems

**Company:** Halyard (fictional)
**Location:** Manchester, UK (hybrid)

## About the role

We are building products on top of large language models and we care more about
whether they behave than about whether they are clever. We are looking for an
engineer who treats a language model as a component to be constrained and
measured, not a magic box to be trusted on faith.

You will own features end to end: the evaluation harness that proves a change is
safe, the guardrails that keep model output inside the lines, and the deployment
that holds up under real traffic.

## What we are looking for

- Strong experience shipping language-model features into production.
- A track record of building evaluation and testing infrastructure, not just using it.
- Fluency in TypeScript or Python and comfort owning backend services.
- A bias toward deterministic, testable design around stochastic components.
- Care for privacy and data handling.

## Nice to have

- Retrieval-augmented generation experience.
- Open-source work we can read.
- Experience mentoring other engineers.

## How we work

Small teams, design reviews, test-driven development, and a strong preference for
making the unhappy path fail loudly in continuous integration rather than quietly
in production.
